My name is Johan Borgström and I am a Designer & Technical Artist. Petfactory was founded with the aim to fuse **Design** with **Animation**. When I code I prefer to do so with Python and VEX.

I studied industrial design at [Umeå Institute of Design](https://www.umu.se/en/umea-institute-of-design/){: target="_blank" } which resulted in a BFA in industrial design. I spent the first year of my masters degree studying interaction design at Umeå Institute of Design. The second year I studied design at [HDK-Valand Academy of Art and Design](https://www.gu.se/en/hdk-valand){: target="_blank" } which resulted in a MFA in design.
